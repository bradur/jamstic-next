Path of the Shaman

Zarguuf has ran out of supplies for his wicked concoctions. He decides to take a quick sprint to the Fungi Forest.
Watch out for boulders and trees. Hitting your big nose will make you want to drop everything and go home.
In story mode Zarguuf has to collect a good amount of all the magic mushrooms to fill his cabinets.
Endless mode goes on until Zarguuf's nose gets a good hit. Try to get a high score for the scoreboard!

Controls

Arrows / WASD - Move Zarguuf.
