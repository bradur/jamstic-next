Sunk Cost

You are a small-time submarine entrepreneur. Recently the waters have become too dangerous, so much so that the locals are too scared to buy your services. You decide to find out the root cause of the problem.

Gameplay

  • Hunt down fish & treasure to gain gold for upgrades
  • Explore the deep waters and find the reason for the rising fish aggression

Keys

Key(s)Action
Arrows / WASDMove
Spacebar / LMBShoot
MouseAim (after relevant upgrade)
Q / EscOpen options / pause
TabOpen shop
