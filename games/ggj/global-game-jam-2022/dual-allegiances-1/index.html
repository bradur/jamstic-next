Dual Allegiances

You live in a small town where humans and wolfmen are fighting an endless war. One day you wake up and realise you have forgotten who you are. A night comes before you manage to reach fellow humans when you notice a change happening. Suddenly you are a wolfman! Which side will you choose? Kill enough of the others to rid yourself of the curse.
WASD to move.
Aim with mouse.
Left click is normal attack.
Right click is a heavy leap attack for werewolf.
Kill enough humans as a werewolf or wolfmen as a human to win!
